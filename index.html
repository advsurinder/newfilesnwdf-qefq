Import customer orders from Excel

Upload an .xlsx, .xls, or .csv file of orders. Each row should have at least a customer name. Any row with a status like "Cancelled," "Error," "Chargeback," "Dispute," or "Refund" — or that simply has something in the customer comment column, or a nonzero error charge — is automatically logged as an incident. That customer will show with a yellow/red name and appear in "View all defaulters" with the reason, amount, restaurant, dasher, and who cancelled/handled it, if that's in your sheet. A blank comment/error charge with no status column is treated as a normal, clean order.

Customers are matched (so re-uploading never creates duplicates) by name + phone first, then by email, and by name alone as a last resort if your sheet has no phone/email columns at all. Orders are matched and merged by DD Order ID specifically — uploading the same file multiple times, or a file with more detail about an order you already imported, updates the existing record instead of duplicating it.
Caveat: name-only matching means two genuinely different customers who happen to share an identical name will be treated as one profile. Adding a phone or email column to your export avoids this entirely.
